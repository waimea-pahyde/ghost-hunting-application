# Project Requirements

## Identified Problem or Need

Local ghost hunters in the Nelson region have a difficult time identifying potentially haunted locations due to our lack of abandoned buildings. 

## End-User Requirements

Replace this text with a description of your typical end-users and their specific needs / expectations / requirements.

My typical end-users will be ghost hunters who need an easy to use website which shows possible haunted locations in and around their specific locations. 

My website will also be used by people looking to report ghost sightings. The website will need to accommodate for those people by making it easy to make a report. 


## Proposed Solution

I will make a website which displays reports of ghost sightings to ghost hunters, making them able to find locations to investigate. My website will also have a form to report ghosts easily accessible from the home page for people wanting to report ghost sightings. 

# Relevant Implications

## Accessability 

Considering accessability means considering that the website will be available and accessible to all people who may want to use the system.  

### Relevance to the System

My website may be used by people with vision problems who may need screen readers.

### Impact / Considerations

I will add alt text to all images, leave a good contrast between text and the background, and a decent font size that most people can read. It could also mean clear fonts for body texts. 

## End user

The system meets the requirements of the end users. 

### Relevance to the System

The system needs to cater to the people who will use it. 

### Impact / Considerations

I will need to consider the needs of local ghost hunters. They may need to use this website while searching for locations on the go, so it must be easy to find locations from the home screen. 


## Usability

Usability means the system must be simple and natural to use. 

### Relevance to the System

The system may be used by many people, so it must be instinctive enough that they want to use it. 

### Impact / Considerations

Ghost hunters have a tendency to move around a lot. BEcause of this, the system will need t obe easily useable so the ghost hunters can use it quickly and effectively. 


## Functionality

The app should be functional with no bugs or broken images. 

### Relevance to the System

My website will be used by people, so the more bug free it is the better. 

### Impact / Considerations

My website will potentially have a map showing ghost locations, which will need to be functional for the ghost hunters. The website will also need to be bug free for maximum efficiency. 


## Aesthetics

Considering aesthetics means that you are not just considering the functionality of the website but also the way it looks. 

### Relevance to the System

If I want people to use my website then I will need to consider making it aesthetically pleasing.

### Impact / Considerations

The website should have attractive design that follows a ghost hunting theme, which could mean less bright colours and more subtle ones.
